🍚 Teriyaki Chicken Poke Bowl

A quick, fresh rice bowl with sticky teriyaki chicken, crunchy veg, edamame and a homemade sauce that feels bright, balanced and a little bit special.

This is one of those meals I keep coming back to when I want something fast but still satisfying. The chicken gets a proper glaze, the toppings bring texture and freshness, and it comes together easily with ingredients you can prep ahead or use straight from the fridge.

Servings

2 servings

Quick Stats

⏱ 25 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 Base: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 300g chicken thigh
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp mirin or rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 cup edamame
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 cup corn
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 tbsp homemade sauce

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken:
    Toss the sliced chicken with soy sauce, honey, mirin or rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
  2. Cook the chicken:
    Pan-fry or grill the chicken until sticky and lightly caramelised.
  3. Prep the toppings:
    Slice the carrot and cucumber, and portion out the edamame, corn, and spring onion.
  4. Assemble the bowl:
    Start with rice, then add the chicken and vegetables. Finish with the sauce and extra spring onion.
  5. Serve:
    Eat straight away while the chicken is warm and the veg still crisp.

Key Nutrients & Benefits

Chicken and edamame provide a solid protein hit, while carrot, cucumber, and corn add crunch, fibre and a good mix of vitamins.

Smart Swaps & Variations

  • Use tofu instead — a great vegetarian option with the same sauce.
  • Add avocado — extra creaminess and healthy fats.
  • Swap the sauce — go spicy with chilli crisp or keep it simple with extra soy and sesame.

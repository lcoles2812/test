🌮 Crispy Chorizo Tacos

Crispy folded tacos packed with smoky chorizo, melted cheese, red onion and coriander, finished with a good squeeze of lime.

These are all about contrast: crunchy golden tortillas, rich spicy filling, and fresh bright toppings to cut through the fat.

Perfect for a quick taco night and easy to scale for a crowd.

Servings

4 servings

Quick Stats

⏱ 25–30 mins • 🔥 Comfort Food • 🍽 Base: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 500g chorizo sausage meat (casings removed)
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1.5 cups grated mozzarella or tasty cheese
  • 1 small red onion, finely diced
  • 0.5 cup coriander leaves, chopped
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 0.5 tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  • 0.5 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt (optional)

Method

  1. Crisp the filling:
    Heat a pan over medium-high heat with olive oil. Cook chorizo, breaking it up, until browned and slightly crispy around the edges.
  2. Warm tortillas:
    Heat tortillas briefly in a dry pan or microwave so they are pliable and easy to fold.
  3. Build tacos:
    Top one side of each tortilla with chorizo and cheese, then fold into a half-moon.
  4. Pan-crisp:
    Return folded tacos to a lightly oiled pan. Cook 2-3 minutes per side until golden and crispy with melted cheese inside.
  5. Top and finish:
    Scatter with red onion, coriander and chilli flakes. Serve with lime wedges and sour cream on the side.

Key Nutrients & Benefits

High in protein and satisfying fats, with fresh herbs and lime to brighten the rich, smoky chorizo filling.

Macros (approx per serve)

  • Calories: 600–760 kcal
  • Protein: 30–38g
  • Carbs: 30–40g
  • Fat: 35–48g

Smart Swaps & Variations

  • Turkey chorizo - Leaner but still smoky and punchy.
  • Add beans - Stretches the filling and adds fibre.
  • Pickled onion - Adds acidity and crunch.
  • Extra heat - Add jalapeno or chipotle sauce.

🍋 Lemon Garlic Chilli Broccolini Pasta with Chicken

Light, fresh pasta with garlic, chilli, lemon and tender chicken. Built for speed, flavour and balance.

⏱ 25 mins 💪 High Protein 🍽 2–3 servings

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Pasta (spaghetti or linguine)
  • Broccolini
  • Garlic cloves
  • Chilli flakes
  • Lemon (zest + juice)
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper

Method

  1. Cook pasta in salted water. Reserve pasta water.
  2. Blanch broccolini for 2–3 mins.
  3. Sear chicken until golden, then slice.
  4. Fry garlic + chilli in olive oil.
  5. Add lemon juice + zest.
  6. Toss pasta + broccolini + pasta water.
  7. Return chicken and mix through.
  8. Finish with parmesan.

Nutrition

High protein, balanced carbs, light fats.

Key Benefits

Vitamin C (lemon), fibre (broccolini), protein (chicken), antioxidants (garlic).

Smart Swaps

Swap chicken → prawns, add spinach, use wholewheat pasta, or add cream for richness.

🍔 Chicken Burger

Crispy crumbed chicken with fresh slaw and a spicy creamy sauce.

Servings

2 servings

Quick Stats

⏱ 25–35 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 Base: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup red cabbage
  • ½ cup carrot
  • ¼ onion
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 3 pickles

Method

  1. Prepare chicken for even cooking:
    Flatten chicken breasts slightly so they cook evenly and stay juicy inside while crisping outside.
  2. Set up crumbing station:
    Prepare three bowls: flour (optional), beaten egg, and breadcrumbs. Coat chicken in egg → breadcrumbs, pressing firmly so the coating sticks well.
  3. Cook the chicken properly:
    Pan-fry or air-fry until golden brown and fully cooked. Let it rest for a few minutes after cooking so the crust stays crisp and juices settle.
  4. Make the slaw (balance + crunch):
    Mix cabbage, carrot, and onion with a bit of Greek yogurt, salt, and vinegar or lemon juice. The goal is fresh, crunchy, and slightly tangy to cut through the fried chicken.
  5. Make the sauce:
    Combine mayo, Greek yogurt, and sriracha. Adjust spice level depending on preference — it should be creamy with a kick.
  6. Assemble the burger:
    Toast the bun if using. Layer slaw first, then crispy chicken, then sauce, and finish with pickles for acidity.
  7. Final balance check:
    Make sure you have crunch (chicken), freshness (slaw), creaminess (sauce), and acidity (pickles) in every bite.

Key Nutrients & Benefits

High protein from chicken supports muscle repair, cabbage and carrot provide fibre and vitamin C for digestion and immunity, and yogurt supports gut health.

Macros (approx per serve)

  • Calories: 450–650 kcal
  • Protein: 30–45g (high, supports satiety)
  • Carbs: 30–50g (breadcrumbs + bun if used)
  • Fats: 15–30g (frying oil + mayo)

Smart Swaps & Variations

  • Air-fry instead of pan-fry — reduces oil while keeping crisp texture.
  • Turkey mince patty — lighter protein option with lower fat.
  • Add spicy slaw — mix in jalapeños or extra sriracha for heat.
  • Wholemeal bun — higher fibre, slower digestion.
  • Extra pickles — increases acidity and cuts through richness.

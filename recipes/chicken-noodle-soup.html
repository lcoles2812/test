🍜 Chicken Noodle Soup

Slow-simmered roast chicken broth with ginger, garlic, miso, and noodles.

Servings

4 servings

Quick Stats

⏱ 2–3 hrs • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 Base: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 whole roast chicken
  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 tbsp miso paste
  • 200g noodles
  • 2 spring onions

Method

  1. Build the stock base:
    Remove meat from the roast chicken and set aside. Place carcass in a large pot with onion, garlic, ginger, and carrot. Cover with water and bring to a gentle simmer.
  2. Slow simmer for depth:
    Let the broth simmer gently for 1–2 hours. Skim off any impurities that rise to the top for a cleaner, clearer broth.
  3. Strain and refine:
    Remove solids and strain the broth. Return the liquid to the pot — this is your flavour base.
  4. Add miso carefully:
    Dissolve miso paste into a ladle of hot broth first, then stir it back in. Avoid hard boiling once miso is added to preserve flavour and probiotics.
  5. Cook noodles:
    Add noodles directly to the broth and cook until just tender. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  6. Add chicken back in:
    Shred the reserved chicken and add it back to the soup. Let it warm through gently.
  7. Finish and balance:
    Taste and adjust seasoning. Add salt or extra miso if needed. Finish with fresh spring onion for brightness and freshness.

Key Nutrients & Benefits

Rich in collagen and amino acids from slow-simmered chicken bones, supporting joint and gut health. Miso provides probiotics for digestion, while garlic and ginger support immune function and inflammation response.

Macros (approx per serve)

  • Calories: 300–450 kcal
  • Protein: 25–40g (high from chicken)
  • Carbs: 30–55g (mainly from noodles)
  • Fats: 8–18g (depending on chicken skin content)

Smart Swaps & Variations

  • Add extra vegetables — spinach, bok choy, or mushrooms increase fibre and nutrients.
  • Use soba or rice noodles — gluten-free or higher-fibre alternatives.
  • Spicy version — add chilli oil or fresh chilli for heat and depth.
  • Extra protein — add soft-boiled egg or tofu.
  • Low-carb version — reduce noodles and increase broth + veg.

🍚 Sticky Beef Mince Bowl

Sweet and savoury glazed beef mince with umami broth over rice.

Servings

2 servings

Quick Stats

⏱ 25–30 mins • 💪 High Protein • 🍽 Base: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 300g beef mince
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp miso paste
  • 250ml chicken stock
  • 200g rice

Method

  1. Caramelise the beef (key step):
    Heat pan until very hot. Add beef mince and press it down. Let it brown properly before stirring — this builds deep flavour. Remove excess liquid if needed.
  2. Build aromatics base:
    Add onion and cook until soft and slightly golden. Add garlic and cook for 30–60 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Create the glaze:
    Add soy sauce and honey. Let it reduce until it becomes sticky and coats the beef — this is the “gloss” layer.
  4. Build umami broth:
    Stir in miso paste and chicken stock. Simmer gently to combine into a light savoury sauce.
  5. Combine and reduce:
    Return beef fully to the pan. Simmer until sauce thickens and clings to mince instead of pooling.
  6. Serve:
    Spoon over steamed rice. Optional: top with spring onion, sesame seeds, or chilli oil for extra depth.

Key Nutrients & Benefits

Beef provides high-quality protein and iron for energy, miso supports gut health through fermentation, while garlic and onion provide immune and antioxidant benefits.

Macros (approx per serve)

  • Calories: 500–700 kcal
  • Protein: 30–40g
  • Carbs: 50–75g (rice-based)
  • Fats: 20–35g

Smart Swaps & Variations

  • Pork mince instead of beef — sweeter, softer texture.
  • Add chilli paste or sriracha — spicy version with heat balance.
  • Add vegetables (carrot, spinach, bok choy) — increases volume and fibre.
  • Swap rice for cauliflower rice — low-carb option.
  • Extra umami — add oyster sauce or soy glaze finish.
